organicflow.pl — full rebuild

A complete ground‑up rewrite of an existing client site. The original had outgrown its stack — slow, brittle, hard to extend. New foundation: Astro on Cloudflare Workers, content modeled in Markdown collections, image pipeline at the edge. Same brand, dramatically faster, and finally a codebase someone besides me can read.

2025 · Side project

Crockpot — recipe search

A search engine for what's in your kitchen. Type ingredients, get real recipes back, ranked by what you already have. Why? Because nobody had built a good one. Full‑text search on a normalized recipe corpus, served from D1 + Workers, with faceted filtering for diet, time, and cuisine.

2024 · Research

yolo‑dualdev — distributed inference

A simplified, public version of the YOLO dual‑device inference work behind my BSc thesis. Splits a single object‑detection pipeline across two devices over a local network — one runs the backbone, the other runs the head — and stitches the result back into a real‑time feed. Originally a lab problem. Now a clean reference implementation.
