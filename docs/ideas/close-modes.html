How to test

  1. Click the card to open the case study.
  2. Click the shrink icon, click the backdrop, or press Esc to close.
  3. Watch the text during the close animation.
  4. Switch modes and repeat.

What to look for

  1. Current: lines re-break as the panel narrows.
  2. Immediate: text gone within ~220ms; only an empty rect shrinks.
  3. Pinned: text stays in its laid-out state; right edge gets clipped.
  4. Both: text fades quickly, no reflow visible.
