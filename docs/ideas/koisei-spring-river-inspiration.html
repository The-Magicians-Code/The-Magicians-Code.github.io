— 01 · The Philosophy

Every spring, the river carries a million petals from the mountains to the sea — and every petal remembers the tree it fell from.

Koisei is a slow descent — from the sakura headwaters above Kyoto to the lantern estuaries of Tokyo. A route drawn by water, printed in woodblock light, and walked one petal at a time.
